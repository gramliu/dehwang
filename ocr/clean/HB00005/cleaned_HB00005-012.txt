SEC. 3. Separability Clause. If, for any reason, any part, section or provision of this
Act is held invalid or unconstitutional, the remaining provisions not affected thereby shall continue
to be in full force and effect.
SEC. 4. Repealing Clause. - All laws, decrees, executive orders, proclamations, rules and
regulations, and other issuances, or parts thereof which are inconsistent with the provisions of this
Act are hereby repealed, amended or modified accordingly.
SEC. 5. Effectivity. This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in
the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
