The administrative expenses of the PIMC during any single fiscal year shall not in any
case exceed five percent (5%) of its total assets.
SEC. 48. Legal Counsel. The Secretary of Justice shall be ex-officio legal adviser of
the PIMC. Any provision of law to the contrary notwithstanding, the PIMC shall have its own
Legal Department, the chief and members of which shall be appointed by the Board of Trustees.
The composition, budget and operating expenses of the Office of the Legal Counsel and the
salaries and traveling expenses of its officers and employees shall be fixed by the Board of
Trustees and paid by the PIMC.
