All other laws, rules and regulations, and other issuances or parts thereof, which are
inconsistent with the provisions of this Act, are hereby repealed, amended, or modified
accordingly.
SEC. 25. Separability Clause. If any provision or part of this Act is declared invalid
or unconstitutional, the remaining parts or provisions not affected shall remain in full force and
effect.
SEC. 26. Effectivity. - This Act shall take effect fifteen (15) days after its publication in
the Official Gazette or in a newspaper of general circulation.
