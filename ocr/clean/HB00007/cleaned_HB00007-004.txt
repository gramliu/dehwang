SEC. 7. Capped Automatic Indexation to Adjustments in Active Personnel Pay.
The adjustment in pension of retired or separated military and uniformed personnel and
survivorship pension of qualified survivors shall be automatically indexed to adjustments in the
salary of military and uniformed personnel in active service holding the same rank: Provided, That
within ten (10) years from the effectivity of this Act, the adjustment in the salary of active
military and uniformed personnel shall not exceed five percent (5%) at any given year and the
adjustment in the pension and survivorship pension shall not exceed five percent (5%) during
the same year.
