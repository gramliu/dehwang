SEC. 2. Declaration of Policy. It is the policy of the State to protect and promote the right to
health of the people and instill health consciousness among them. The State also mandates the
adoption of an integrated and comprehensive approach to health development.
Towards this end, the State shall give high priority to the allocation of material and institutional
resources to protect public health by ensuring that the Philippine health system is well-prepared to
forecast, prevent, monitor, and control diseases, injuries, and disabilities both of national and
international concern.
